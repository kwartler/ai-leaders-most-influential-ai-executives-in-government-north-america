Adapting to the AI Landscape

How can I continue my education with AI specializations in Government?

Explore public sector AI programs from the Partnership on AI, Harvard Kennedy School's digital government courses, or Georgetown's Technology Policy program. Federal AI certifications from organizations like ATARC provide government-specific training.

What conferences should I attend for Government AI?

Key events include GovAI Summit, the ACT-IAC Emerging Technology Conference, ATARC AI Summit, and the Government AI Coalition meetings. These conferences connect public sector AI leaders with technology providers and policy makers.

How can I grow as an AI leader within Government?

Government AI leaders grow by contributing to federal AI strategy development, participating in interagency AI working groups, and building public-private partnerships that advance responsible AI adoption. Publishing policy papers on AI governance demonstrates thought leadership.
